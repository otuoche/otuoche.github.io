Places started as a social media account in 2016. All it had was the passion for showcasing various locations in Nigeria. Growing up in Nigeria, you’ll find out about new places from other people, but what if you could find out from a stranger online with a similar interest.


In 2017, we launched a website that further promoted the vision and over time, the brand built into an ad hub with more plans to make editorials, blog posts, reviews and more.

Places aims to improve adventure and help people discover and visit more places and have more fun. Places was born out of the want for more. More spots to discover, cultures to explore, information around us, more connection to people with like minds, MORE ADVENTURE.

Our Story

Places started as a social media account in 2016. All it had was the passion for showcasing various locations in Nigeria. Growing up in Nigeria, you’ll find out about new places from other people, but what if you could find out from a stranger online with a similar interest.


In 2017, we launched a website that further promoted the vision and over time, the brand built into an ad hub with more plans to make editorials, blog posts, reviews and more.
